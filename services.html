CORPORATE SETUP

MANOHA Tax can setup a corporation, partnership or limited liability company for you. There are many tax advantages for corporations, LLCs or partnerships that do not apply to individual taxpayers. Along with the creation of the business entity, we will apply for an employer identification number on your behalf which can be used to file your income taxes and any other tax documentation such as W2s.
